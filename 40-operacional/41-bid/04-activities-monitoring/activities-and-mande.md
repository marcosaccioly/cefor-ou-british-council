**Os 4 objetivos** (email da Vanessa, pós-corte do antigo obj 3):
1. **Exchange expertise** between OU/IET and Ifes/CEFOR (instructional design, QA, learner support, accessibility, digital education, AI in education, micro-credentials, learning analytics, personalized & adaptive learning).
2. **Identify regionally specialised expertise** across selected Ifes campuses (sustainability, environmental education, culturally diverse communities), with potential for wider online delivery and future TNE.

4. **Co-develop a framework and roadmap** for future online/TNE initiatives, embedding inclusion, equity and sustainability.

## Activity 1 — Distance-learning capability diagnostic · *Diagnóstico de capacidades em EaD* → **Obj 1**
- **Type:** mutual mapping / capability exchange (exploratory)
- **Description:** IET and CEFOR jointly survey and compare their distance-education practices — instructional design, quality assurance, learner support, accessibility, digital education, AI in education, micro-credentials, learning analytics, and personalized/adaptive learning — identifying convergences and unexplored areas.
- **Deliverable:** joint IET × CEFOR capability diagnostic report (open access).
- **Indicator:** practice areas mapped on both sides; ≥`TBD` convergence/gap areas identified and validated by both teams.
- **Means of verification:** published report; validation sign-off by both institutions.
- **Indicative timing:** months 1–4.

## Activity 2 — Thematic mapping of Ifes campuses · *Mapeamento temático dos campi* → **Obj 2**
- **Type:** context mapping (institutional/regional)
- **Description:** structured survey with representatives of selected Ifes campuses on initiatives in inclusion, diversity, gender, environment and sustainability — to identify regionally specialised expertise with potential for future online (EaD) delivery and TNE. Vulnerable/diverse communities (e.g. indigenous, coastal/"economia do mar", environmental education) are mapped here as **future audiences**, not as a research object.
- **Deliverable:** thematic map of regionally specialised expertise across campuses.
- **Indicator:** ≥`TBD` campuses engaged; ≥`TBD` specialised expertise areas identified, with an explicit gender/EDI lens.
- **Means of verification:** mapping document; participation records of campuses/representatives.
- **Indicative timing:** months 3–8.

## Activity 3 — Experience exchange: workshops, OU visit & Community of Practice · *Trocas de experiências* → **Obj 3**
- **Type:** capacity building / relationship building (exploratory)
- **Description:** online experience-exchange workshops in distance education between IET and CEFOR; a **5-day in-person visit to the OU** for immersion in IET's practices and structures; and the creation of an **IET–CEFOR Community of Practice** as a continuity mechanism.
- **Deliverable:** workshop series + records; OU visit report; established Community of Practice; **inclusive-curriculum tool** (output cited by Vanessa).
- **Indicator:** ≥`TBD` workshops delivered; visit completed with ≥`TBD` participants; CoP active with ≥`TBD` members; inclusive-curriculum tool produced.
- **Means of verification:** workshop materials/attendance; visit report; CoP charter/platform; tool artifact.
- **Indicative timing:** months 5–12 (OU visit ~months 8–10).

## Activity 4 — Co-design of framework and roadmap · *Co-design do framework e roadmap* → **Obj 4**
- **Type:** co-design (framework + strategy)
- **Description:** collaborative construction of (a) a joint OU–Ifes **framework** to expand access to regionally specialised expertise through distance education; and (b) a strategic **roadmap** identifying future opportunities for collaboration in online education and TNE between the two institutions, embedding inclusion, equity and sustainability.
- **Deliverable:** joint OU–Ifes framework document + strategic TNE roadmap.
- **Indicator:** framework and roadmap reviewed and **endorsed by both institutions**.
- **Means of verification:** versioned documents; endorsement record (minute/sign-off).
- **Indicative timing:** months 10–15.

## Activity 5 — Dissemination · *Disseminação* → *(transversal)*
- **Type:** dissemination / communication
- **Description:** dissemination of results through joint articles submitted to journals and scientific events, and communication via social media and institutional channels (e.g. CONCEFOR/CONIF where applicable).
- **Deliverable:** ≥`TBD` joint article(s) submitted; communication pieces across channels.
- **Indicator:** ≥`TBD` submissions; ≥`TBD` communication outputs.
- **Means of verification:** submission records; published communications.
- **Indicative timing:** months 12–17 (continuous from mid-project).
